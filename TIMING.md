# Timing notes: Glass Animals - Creatures in Heaven

**Source audio:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BdE12N5heG0

### Timing

This song is at a constant tempo of **80 beats per minute** (andante/adagietto) and in common (4/4) time.

In other units:

* Each beat is 750 ms.
* Each measure is 3000 ms (4 beats per measure).

Timing is kept extremely precisely in this song and should be close to a round number. Off-syncs would be caused by delays, not tempo drift.

Approx. 5 seconds of the source audio's intro was trimmed as foley/background noise for the music video.

To simplify timekeeping, a small amount of silence was added to the tail of the audio clips to extend them to be precisely 4 minutes and 30 seconds long, or 90 measures long, or 360 beats long.
